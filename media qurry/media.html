Web Development

Web development involves creating and maintaining websites. It includes front-end design, back-end programming, and database management.

Web Design

Web design involves creating visually appealing, user-friendly websites using layout, color, typography, and interactivity to enhance user experience.

Marketing

Marketing is promoting products or services through research, strategy, advertising, and communication to attract, engage, and retain customers effectively.

Support

A support system offers emotional strength, guidance, encouragement, and stability through relationships, helping individuals navigate challenges and achieve growth.
